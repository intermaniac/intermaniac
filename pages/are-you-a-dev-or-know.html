Are you a dev or know someone hyper about building a DApp? These are the five finest @GitHub repos & kits to get started. ↓ A thread ↓

1/ Web3 Starter by @shivkanthb will get give you a simple web3 starter with @MetaMask and @WalletConnect. You can clone the repo here ↓ https://t.co/CS5SOwU7Jr

2/ Stylized web3 modal themes by @traf will get you a step further with a couple different options to make your DApp look even better from the start ↓ https://t.co/kLT7gW5awS

3/ If you are experienced and don't mind going a bit overboard, then this is definitely for you. In the words of @NoahZinsmeister, this is a simple, maximally extensible, dependency-minimized framework for building modern Ethereum dApps ↓ https://t.co/jafrU0eZs8

4/ If you are looking for robust & extensive kits created by teams of developers, then @family's: https://t.co/RFc2QVNlTK & @rainbowdotme's: https://t.co/Z6AuLqtpkE are the right pick for you ↓

5/ Next up is a wagmi, a collection of React Hooks containing everything you need to start working with Ethereum by @awkweb. This will help you out along the way ↓ https://t.co/a4UpfngrAo

And that's that. What other repos or kits are you using? We are committed to the kit by @rainbowdotme for our wallet authentication @belonghaus. And we absolutely love it. Found this helpful? Let's stay connected via @itsjulianpaul
