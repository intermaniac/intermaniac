How to avoid top 5 mistakes in SQL Thread:

2. Use EXISTS() Instead of COUNT() - If you want to know whether a record/result exists or not, it is better to use exists(), rather than count()

- count(), will browse the entire table to get you the number of records. - Whereas, exists(), will get back to you, when it finds the first record for the query, saving you time and computing.
