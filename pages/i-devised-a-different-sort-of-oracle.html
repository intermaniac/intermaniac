Let's roll on Decay-Growth, and interpret it as phases of the day. I roll a 5, so it is sunrise! From that, I could define wind with Weak-Strong, the skies with Open-Close, and so on. We can use the same strategy to determine a person's demeanor, reaction, appearance... üßµ
