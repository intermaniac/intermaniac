Hey frens! 👋 It's not smth new that @GrantShares pioneered #grants #DAOs bringing, e.g., "leadership committee made up of individuals/entities", "multi-sig wallet controlled by the protocol", "public discussions", "integration to @GitHub", "on-chain proposals", etc. 1/8 🧵👇

What is new for us is that other large players in the market are following the way we envisaged things! E.g., @ConsenSys' @MetaMask #grants #DAO Check it out 👇 https://t.co/NROIn7QY3o 2/8

Some of the governance properties designed by @ConsenSys' @MetaMask #grants #DAO are indeed super similar to what we have on production right now.🤯😅🛠️ This was part of our vision, which started 1.5 years ago. 3/8

What @MetaMask #grants #DAO calls an "employee-led program", we (@GrantShares) call a "community-driven program". The communities that bootstrapped @GrantShares #DAO are making decisions on behalf of who provided funds (initially, in our case, @Neo_Blockchain Foundation). 4/8

☝️this offloads the decision-making of community growth through #grants from the funder (e.g., @ConsenSys, @Neo_Blockchain Foundation, etc) to individuals/entities with skin in the game ("leadership committee"). They know the best in which areas grants should be issued. 🤓 5/8

well... this☝️can be seen as some sort of "delegation". The important part is that @GrantShares #DAO also supports adding or removing members of the "leadership committee" -- only through proposals. And, actually, anyone can submit a proposal! https://t.co/i5aUjjIjd6 6/8

At the same time we're happy to see more #grants #DAO emerging in specific ecos, we genuinely believe in plurality and connections across them. Meaning: having a #grants #DAO platform for multiple projects, chains, and communities. We're actively thinking about this! 🚀🌱 7/8

If you're interested in experimenting with #grants #DAOs, join us! Follow us⭐️and drop us a line! Check out 👇 https://t.co/D6jdnOYxZz and https://t.co/OHsSwyB0FU 8/8
