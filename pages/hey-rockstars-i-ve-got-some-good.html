Hey, rockstars 💜 I’ve got some good news 🗞️ After 65 days of being unemployed as a DevRel, I’m pleased and excited to let you all know that I’m now a JavaScript engineer and DevRel at @treblleapi Here is a thread of the journey to this role 🧵👇🏾

How I got the job is a funny story and @DavorKolenc can attest to it 😂. Here is a thread on the gist 🧵👇🏾

When I lost my previous DevRel job I went into brute force mode trying to secure another job as quickly as I was let go(sapa na your mate 😹).

I did interviews and I was loved but the job wasn’t coming through for some reason...

After a couple of those, I decided to focus on what I love which is teaching JavaScript. I decide to go all in on Twitter, YouTube, my blog, and https://t.co/Cym2j1LSnf.">https://t.co/Cym2j1LSnf.

So to make ends meet(a brother has got bills to pay), I decided to try my hands at sponsored videos. Coincidentally, @treblleapi came on my radar and since I’m a big fan of APIs I decided they’d make a great first sponsor for TKYT.

So I reached out to @DavorKolenc who was more than willing to meet.

We met, and a few minutes into the conversation, what was planned to be a business meeting felt like two pals catching up after a long while 😅

We talked about Treblle, Vedran the CEO, and how he loves Laravel and everything else but the actual sponsorship 😅

I also spoke nonstop of my interests in APIs and my love for JavaScript and Davor then mentioned I should interview for a DevRel role at Treblle.

I didn’t think much of it because I was weary of interviews at this point but he set up a meeting with the CEO and the rest is history...

I tried out Treblle before the meeting with the CEO out of curiosity and ended up integrating it into https://t.co/Cym2j1LSnf and made a @sailsjs hook to make integrating Treblle and Sails a breeze. You can check it out 👇🏾 https://t.co/Aqi1f7FnPn

Vedran, the CEO saw this and was amazed by the package and made this tweet 👇🏾 https://t.co/kVG1iWFmAJ

So you all know I’ve been talking about APIs but get ready I’m now going to be paid to talk about it some more on both this account and @treblleapi so you better follow us both 😹 as Treblle API will make your API integrations and monitoring a breeze 💪🏾

And that's not all, not only will I be talking about APIs, part of my job will be building and maintaining the open-source JavaScript SDKs we have at Treblle. Aren't I lucky! I'll be getting paid to work on JavaScript, open-source, and APIs. Is this heaven 😋 https://t.co/BhjkG0qabp

I’d like to thank my buddy @loige who was there for me when I was still sulking over the loss of the job 😅

Also @praneet_ro, @simonswis, and @jamesqquick, who literally mentioned I was looking for a job on his YouTube (amazing guy) You all rock 💜

And of course my family, but I doubt they’d see this, or will they 👀? Especially my little bro who tried to cheer me up when I lost my previous job in the way he thought best but ended up pissing me off 😂

Alright so if you want to follow on in this new exciting voyage of mine, give me(@Dominus_Kelvin) a follow, and let's stay connected. I'm also looking out to your tweets on your successes rockstars and do well to tag me as they happen.
