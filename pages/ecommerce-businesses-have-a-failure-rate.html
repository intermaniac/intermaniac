ECommerce businesses have a failure rate of about 80%-90%. Let’s check this thread to unveil why a business fails and tips to handle that! #ecommerce #ecommercewebsite #wordpress #wordpresswebsite #ecommercesite #onlineshopping #onlinebusiness

Mistake #1: Not Doing Proper Research Before Starting Solution: 1. Select the type of product you want to sell 2. Analyze your market size, type, & competitors 3. Prepare an effective eCommerce business plan 4. Choose the right platform to build your store

Mistake #2: Using Pop-ups & Other Advertisements Wrongly Solution: 1. Include powerful words to highlight special offers/benefits 2. Display personalized popups based on user segmentation 3. Show exit-intent popup 4. Use numbers & CTA buttons for quick response

Mistake #3. Ignoring Bad Reviews Solution: Sometimes your users can post negative reviews. Instead of ignoring them, you should reply with a gentle apology and promise to make your service better next time. You can also offer special discounts for their inconvenience.

Mistake #4. Not Doing Any Experiment or A/B Testing Solution: In eCommerce, you need to pay attention to business cycles & visitor behaviors. Based on this data, apply some changes or A/B tests. By monitoring the results of these experiments you can bring fruitful advancements.

These are the common problems that you should always be aware of to be successful in your eCommerce business. To get a case study on how a big eCommerce business failed, check this blog - https://t.co/wiUwz8kAFk
