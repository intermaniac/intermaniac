Customers don't do business with your product; their business is with YOU! Is your #CustomerExperience plan ready for it? - A thread on stellar CX plan #WordPress https://t.co/sbXQc1ZbyX

1. Create a customer-friendly website Your website is like your digital reception. Slow page speeds and moldy walls will surely kill the experience. Build a website that's fast and responsive. Keep these in mind; https://t.co/fcjym58JiC

2. Optimise your product pages The obvious goal of any eCommerce website is to drive online sales. A compelling product page makes all the difference between a sale and a quick bounce.

3. Diversify your presence Selling at more places means selling to more people. It's best for you to diversify your presence in the world of digital commerce. After all, the more sales channels you use, the more customers you can reach.

4. Introduce a simple checkout A truly customer-friendly store must have an intuitive and SIMPLE checkout system. Reduce the number of checkout steps to 4 & include a visual progress indicator, so customers know their purchase is near complete.

5. Go Mobile people More than 70% of searches are done on Mobiles. If your website can't handle mobile compatibility, that's 70% of potential searches lost to competitors. Check mobile responsiveness obsessively, you'll thank me later!
