Identify the Problem here: The main problem here is that you want to implement a heck a lot of features in your code, or want to make it better. Trust me, small steps are better than a huge fall!

Steps to Take: 1) Keep track of a To-Do list where you have written down what to implement 2) Do one task at a time 3) After completing the one task, publish it to your actual codebase or release it, since you can add more features later.

The mistake I did! I am Making a Deal Finder app, that helps you find better deal for a given product by searching it in that Platform or even somewhere else. I tried to implement a ton of features and tried to make my code efficient.

I was having such a hard time with my OCD, that I scraped the project and Did it again and again. Finally, I understood and now, I am trying to complete the first feature and then Release it.

Remember, when you show hope to yourself, that the project is going to end soon, you will never slow down. Just as I said, If you keep track of notes and make checkpoints/goals, your brain will help you to do the work even more effectively.
