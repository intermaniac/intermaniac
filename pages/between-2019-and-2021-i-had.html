Between 2019 and 2021, I had a really active WordPress blog. When I was actively blogging, I would publish really awesome, really useful posts, and people would come in my comments and DMs to thank me for sharing useful stuff with them. #Thread

For a while, I told them they were welcome and accepted all the goodwill. After a while, I began to think to myself, "Should they really be thanking me?" or "Should they be thanking only me?" The next time I posted and people loved it and thanked me, I started to tell them...

... to thank themselves too. Why? 99% of the people who consumed my content were people who had clicked on the links to my posts on WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and Twitter. Not many people scrolled on the internet and just stumbled on my posts.

They needed to thank themselves for seeing something valuable and making the decision to check through and update their knowledge or take advantage of the opportunities I had shared.
