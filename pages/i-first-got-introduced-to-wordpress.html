I first got introduced to WordPress in 2013, when the freelancer I hired to build a website for my business pulled the wool over my eyes. Not one to let a good crisis go to waste, I decided to build the damn thing myself! There was one small problem though... A THREAD

I didn't know the first thing about building websites. So, I took an online course on https://t.co/TlATGBZ7fy and taught myself the basics! And I've never stopped learning since. Continued...

Almost a decade later, I'm still in the trenches. As a hobbyist, I've tried my hand at almost every "make money online" scheme under the sun. Here are some of the most "noteworthy": Continued...

- I built over a dozen WordPress sites (a directory, a news aggregator site, a couple of blogs, a few business sites...you name it!) and even managed to sell a few. Continued...

- I built a one-page test website for a totally fictional "motorcycle rental business" and ranked it on page 1 of Google (https://t.co/Uxut7e6igw) for high buyer intent keywords...then got bombarded with phone calls to rent bikes I didn't even own. Continued...

- I freelanced as a copywriter on Fiverr and became a Level 2 seller with over 50 5-star ratings. Continued...

- I launched https://t.co/ZRR3ZGQJRV, my own brand of CBD food supplements, and built the WooCommerce store for it. My first batch of stock sold out, but I couldn't afford the 25K restock and had to shelve the damn thing. Continued...

- I went DEEP down the Cold Email rabbit hole. But after 6 months of repeatedly getting my mailing domains blacklisted and my emailing accounts shut down, I threw in the towel. I never got told to f-ck off in so many different ways, all via email of course. Continued...

I won't mention the number of "get rich quick" courses I paid for. Turns out the best way to get rich quickly, is to sell courses to other people teaching them how to get rich quickly. You live, you learn. Continued...

The key takeaway from the last 10 years is that there are no shortcuts to success. Slow and steady wins the race. And, if you focus on providing value to your "customer", the rest generally takes care of itself. That's why I've nailed my colors to the SEO mast. Continued...

I'm currently building 2 niche websites, monetized with ads & affiliate marketing. I've been eating, breathing, and sleeping SEO for the past 12 months solid. Continued...

What I believe gives me an edge is that I can draw on my 3rd-level qualifications in business & marketing, 10+ years of experience in consumer goods marketing with market-leading brands, and multiple entrepreneurial ventures, to inform and enrich my passion for SEO. Continued...

All I need now is an opportunity. So, if you need an SEO with a story, hit me up!
