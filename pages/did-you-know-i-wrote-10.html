I thought Python 101 would be the ONLY book I'd write, but my readers kept asking for a sequel! Python 201: Intermediate Python was born in 2016 and ended up being pretty popular too, for an indie author such as myself. https://t.co/baTVpfLxLX

wxPython Cookbook was my 3rd book and it came in Jan. 2017 I like the scene, but I wish I had made the #Python more prominent in this one This book is no longer for sale as it got bought and reworked by Apress https://t.co/SjaKxSo0Si

wxPython Recipes came out in December 2017. I learned a few things about publishers and editing and royalties. https://t.co/3rlyn6pQi3

Python Interviews came out in Feb 2018 from Packt Publishing. It was fun to talk with so many #Python experts https://t.co/onTYk60fpo

ReportLab: PDF Processing with Python is what I consider to be my 5th book. It came out in June 2018 and was one of my favorites to write. I also really like how the cover art came out on this one! https://t.co/VxTlNRhyeT

2018 was a BIG year for me with Jupyter Notebook 101 being the 3rd book I released that year in October. This is my 6th book. While the pre-sales were kind of lousy, this book sold fairly consistently on Amazon for a couple of years https://t.co/UfTpZwTHkQ

My 7th book was Creating GUI Applications with wxPython. Another of my favorites to write and the art is really fun! https://t.co/lPcogZCKyH

Python 101: 2nd Edition came out in Aug. 2020. It was a COMPLETE rewrite of the original book and is in many ways a combination of the original 101 and 201 books https://t.co/nx3OYVbhnH

My 9th #Python book was called Pillow: Image Processing with Python and came out in March 2021 This book is my ONLY full-color book in print, which is neat, but $$$ I don't really make much of anything on paperback sales, but it's a cool book! https://t.co/hQjOR64jcd

My 10th #Python book is called Automating Excel with Python This is a super cool book too! https://t.co/QtMHo7ILkb

What book will be my 11th #Python book? That remains to be seen. I have at least 3 or 4 ideas that I have been kicking around. Hopefully, I can get one of those ideas out yet this year!
