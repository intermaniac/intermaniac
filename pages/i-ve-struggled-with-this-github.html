I’ve struggled with this @GitHub “issue” (#lulz!) for years and feel no closer to a good answer. TL/DR: #mobile #backlog management is still in fantasy-land! ☘️🦄🛸 Details in the thread and if you have answers… please… #epic #coder 🧵 https://t.co/8WwUCdKkyN

I’m probably a lot like you, if you’ve been writing #software for four decades. 🦉 I participate in many projects, at different levels, across many organizations. 🤹 I have more than one @GitHub identity because, you know, #crypto. 🤐 https://t.co/UvT8pn2tbl

I’m a dedicated #Agile practitioner, because it’s 2022 and I’m not a freaking moron. And #OG ~= #designer/#architect, so I often have a thought while… seated… that needs to land on a #backlog RIGHT NOW before my next thought wipes it out. So to speak. 🤣😂🤣 https://t.co/vy1mRhpUsN

I love @GitHub #issues! Who wouldn’t? It just makes SENSE to keep your #backlog of #requirements right next to the #code that expresses them. I mean I don’t have to tell you what fills up any gap between requirements & code, but you can grow mushrooms in it. 💩🍄 https://t.co/Ctq0VUUhDh

@GitHub #issues aren’t perfect. For one, it’s next to impossible to organize them into #epics and march them across a #kanban board INSIDE the GitHub application. And if you mention GitHub Projects to me right now, you freaking DESERVE them. 🙄 https://t.co/CX5jSKopVl

So in order to make @GitHub #issues REALLY work, you need to layer on another application that can organize & track your issues AND keep them in sync with GitHub. GitHub Projects fanboys kindly #stfu and #stfd, this is at least mostly your fault. 🙄🙄🙄 https://t.co/GddNuqk8Lk

There are applications that do a pretty good job of organizing your @GitHub issues into an #Agile #backlog. Not shilling but I like @zubeio: works well, great #freemium option, and critically Zube supports multiple GitHub identities! 💪😎👍 https://t.co/bLeH5GZNas

Sadly, @zubeio doesn’t have a #MobileApp, and their #WebApp just doesn’t play well on a #MobileDevice. 📵 In fact I’m not aware of ANY mobile app that enhances @GitHub issues with an #Agile overlay AND supports multiple GitHub repos/orgs/ACCOUNTS. https://t.co/ULS3yaT2zo

So… time to recalibrate my #requirements. What do I REALLY need? 🤔 It would be nice to do everything above ☝️ in a #MobileApp… but the one thing I need MOST is to add new @GitHub issues to an #AgileBacklog with high efficiency. ANY backlog. In ANY account. https://t.co/sPTGUVjoEi

So what magic #MobileApp lets me compose an issue and throw it into any @GitHub issues #backlog I can access, on the go, with absolutely ZERO #friction? I wish I knew, and I would definitely pay 💰💰💰 to use it! Any helpful ideas? Please RT & comment! 🪡 https://t.co/g88jhjeMcm
