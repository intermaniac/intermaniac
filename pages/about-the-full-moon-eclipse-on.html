First of all, it’s on a Tuesday (Mars’ day) where it’s not witnessing Scorpio/Taurus. Mars is also RX, so it adds some “re-visiting conflict” to the day.

Mercury is in the heart of this eclipse, trying to have its cazimi (exact 6hrs after the eclipse goes exact) as it’s within 30’ so it’s trying to be its most mercurial self as it restarts its dance with the sun, but the moon is interrupting a bit as it’s a total Lunar Eclipse.

So with the moon going through it’s own cycle of death/rebirth while at it’s fullest stage, and mercury going through a similar cycle- it’s time to reevaluate what holds meaning to you, what’s truly important?

The Decans activated are the Sun/Jupiter part of Scorpio and the Moon/Mercury part of Taurus. This is the hard labor necessary for creating relationships/values that might be a little mundane/repeat I’ve at times, but is entirely necessary.

This is all a pretty close orb to a T-square with Saturn which is going to be more-so affecting the ♉︎ placements (as ♒︎ is superior to ♉︎, yet inferior to ♏︎) so natural law/growth & maturity will be the mediator to this eclipse.

A question that Saturn seems to ask in all of this is “what’s limiting you from becoming a better version of yourself and truly appreciating what you have in front of you?” It brings in unknown info/info from different perspectives (☿/☿ decan ♒︎)

Now Venus rules Taurus, and is stuck in Scorpio. However; it’s the part of Scorpio in which Venus saves Face and is able to be integrate ideas on a deeper, more complex level and truly diving in deep to the well of human emotion in order to have those realizations.

Imo this eclipse is a deep look into the darker sides of our individual nature in order to bring thoughtfulness and understanding into a healthy maintaining of self. It’s a “grow tf up” kinda transit where you have to let go of being petty and just live in the moment.
