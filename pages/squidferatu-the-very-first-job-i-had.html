Squidferatu Thread! The very first job I had on SB was getting to pose almost every Nosferatu in the episode! As a big Monty Python fan it was a ton of fun to tap into my inner Terry Gilliam and bring him to (after)life for his most extensive role on the show. (1/) https://t.co/vpLMPGz3e4

I was given a BIG folder of stock Nosferatu photos that were taken for his last appearance in The Night Patty, and it was a really fun puzzle trying to figure out what to use for this one. And I'm honestly impressed every one of them made it into the final cut! (2/)

I was very new to both Storyboard Pro and drawing for the show at the time, so any new drawings I made are pretty rough. But Squid's become my favorite to draw to this day and this episode still has some of my favorite faces for him - and hey that Slappy is mine too! (3/) https://t.co/3tF6Mhp7YG

More Squidward and Nosferatu acting I contributed - along with Slappy slithering sneakily away at the very end (Bobs kept from the rough board by @/dbexrealinsane and Most Other Slappys by @/TweetinV)! (4/) https://t.co/ngY77ucszo

The "cheese ball" bit during the dinner scene has some of my favorite Squidward faces I've drawn for the show. (5/) https://t.co/7SqJif82Ax

It wouldn't have fit the episode's spooky atmosphere but there are some moments when Nosferatu looks at the camera that I wish had a laughtrack added - like when he gets squirted with ketchup here (his post-blush belch from earlier was another). (6/) https://t.co/AL7E12aJzu

Incidentally, I also cobbled together the Bat Fancy cover. (7/) https://t.co/cTtaEa61FP

The bedroom scene (boarded by @/PinkieToons) was my favorite scene to add Nosferatu to - for making his coming out of the wall increasingly silly, and for his making "Whuh-oh" faces at the camera each time Bob returns. (8/) https://t.co/4AlG1uhbZh
