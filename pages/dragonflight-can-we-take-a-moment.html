#Dragonflight Can we take a moment to please really consider how emotional the Azure Span story regarding Kalecgos, Malygos and Sindragosa is? I talked about this briefly while streaming the other day, but gonna take a few moments here to get into it a bit more ðŸ§µ

We have Kalecgos, who has lost his father figure, his friends and family, even his love (estranged with Jaina). He is struggling to define himself. And he is the only one among the blues to hear the call of Tyrhold and return. And immediately thrust into trying to fix things.

His reaction upon meeting Sindragosa's simulacrum was truly something. He has a chance to connect with someone from his flight's past. Someone who casts a long shadow from the life she lived and the ignoble death she suffered. You can almost FEEL his wounds opening again.

And the best thing is that she accepts him without question. She senses that he is an Aspect and that this means Malygos is dead. Think about it. For millennia the dragonflights each had a single leader who never changed. She has never known another Blue Aspect than Malygos.

But she recognizes and senses his internal struggles and helps him with whatever she needs. Sure, she is just a replica of the real Sindragosa. But she has all her memories and intellect, so she's not so far off as you may think. And Kalecgos rises to the occasion too.

The moment when Kalecgos is facing off against Raszageth and you just KNOW that more Blues HAVE to come to help save him. And then we get the Legion squad back: Senegos, Emmigosa, Stellagosa. It's a moment of triumph. If you quested in Legion, you know these characters.

And the great thing is that we have three distinct generations of Blues represented: Senegos is one of the oldest dragons still alive, Stella is a contemporary to Kalecgos, and Emmi is the future of the flight. And they stand together with Kalecgos against Raszageth. Symbolism!!

Then we have the side quests in Camp Antonidas as some Kirin Tor apprentices discover LOVE POEMS WRITTEN ABOUT MALYGOS AND SINDRAGOSA. Don't know about yall, but it felt epic and emotional to me. For anyone even superficially familiar with the Blues' history, this is a moment.

Sindragosa's painful death + Neltharion's betrayal are the root causes of Malygos's insanity. We take moments here to discover something artistic about the Blues, very unexpected because it's just something you'd never think about. And there's more reasons why its significant.

The only other significant relationship (lore check?) we've seen between dragons , in the husband/wife sense, is with Alexstrasza and Korialstrasz. And that ended in tragedy when Korial died during very unfortunate circumstances in Cataclysm. Their love was potent too.

The great thing is that we've seen quite a bit of their love and relationship in the game and now we're seeing it again with Malygos and Sindragosa. Albeit they are both dead now. But the fact remains that the love between them is part of the very fabric of the zone.

The tragedy of Malygos and Sindragosa, their love for each other, immortalized in the love poems written by a Blue dragonspawn, is a highlight of the Azure Span questing. For an expansion supposed to be about Dragons, the Azure Span questing definitely delivers in spades.
