Twitter is an incredible place to learn. Here's a thread on the top 5 Tweeters on #CX and #CustomerExperience you can't skip! #WordPress

1/5 @Hyken He's the senior guru and a NYT bestseller you have to follow for the latest on #CX. He's been talking about #CX on his podcast and I've found some awesome insight just by listening to him.

2/5 @milescthomas He's got his own book on Customer Experience that's already on my reading list. If you need practical and day-to-day insight, he's the guy to follow!

3/5 @Tiffani_Bova She's a WSJ bestseller and the front hitter for @salesforce Her power to turn #CX into #customersuccess and #growth is mind-boggling. Follow her for insights and the occasional talk on her site.

4/5 @5Tacos Enough with the big profiles. Mr. Ortega has been consistently generating actionable content on CX from unique perspectives. The underdog that you just have to follow!

5/5 @CXpert I found Ben at the very beginning of starting my Twitter journey. When posting #CX and #CustomerService content seemed futile, he gave the motivation to carry on and post the content that resonates with me and my audience. Give him a follow if #CX is your thing.

Shout out to @rednishat & @YourSupportGuy for making my journey interesting and worthwhile.
