6. DAOs or Decentralized Autonomous Organizations are similar in many ways to NFTs as they are also operated via smart contract. These DAOs can be hard coded for the purpose of community organization and action.

7. In simplest terms, you can think of them as an amped up Facebook group. People can crowdfund, plan events, vote, and even gain new members. The best part is, each person has the same amount of say in what the group does.

8. Because of their decentralized nature, DAOs are operated by voting either using NFTs or DAO tokens. Likewise the treasury of a DAO (where money goes) can be set up outside the control of any one individual.

11. They find 3 properties to buy and put the decision to a vote. Each person who owns a token can place a vote and with all the votes tallied they choose property number 2. Once said property is purchased, all management decisions can now be handled by the group.

12. Through the use of a DAO, a group of people was able to crowdfund, come to consensus and make a decision that affects all who participate. From this point, profits made from the property can be shared among token holders.
