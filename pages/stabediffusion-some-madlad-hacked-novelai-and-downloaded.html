ðŸ§µ#StabeDiffusion Some madlad hacked NovelAI and downloaded their git repository for their proprietary models. ~50GB. Apparently he burned a zero-day on this. What a legend. Not much more info besides a PGP-signed he shared stating that "part 2 will come weekend probably".

"In total is ~100GB; contains more SD models + what i think are GPT models. couldnt get everything - novelai cut me off"

"previous checkpoints/epochs of the anime ones also a beta furry one that i couldnt get [in the first part]". If what he's saying is true, NovelAI is losing the foundations of their business: their GPT2 text models.

Is this the power of open-source software? I be this guy is unhappy about NovelAI locking access to their models behind a paywall. All this talk about "democratizing" neural networks but only one person who actually does it.

https://t.co/tyJ3AmFIKR

#StableDiffusion Ok, so things are moving quickly. There are the two different models in the leak: full and pruned. The full unpruned models are used in NAI, which we know because they match the hash in exif data from images generated my NAI.

There are a lot of hypernetworks included. Hypernetworks are used to guide/set weights and are related to GANs. There's a bunch of textual inversion files.

There's also a file called https://t.co/QsYf7Jxnqh No one really knows what it does. They've managed to load it into SD but that's about it. It's used transform the prompt but for what isn't known yet. The API option to enable it is called mitigate.

I think both the float16 and float32 models are present. Right now, only the pruned models can be loaded.
