1) DEFI 101. -introduction -blockchain Oracle. -Defi lending and borrowing -Decentralized exchanges -Derivatives. -Yield Farming. -Prediction . -Assets management. -flash loans. A thread

2)Defi simply means decentralized finance,a variety of applications or projects on the blockchain geared towards disrupting the traditional finance system which is highly centralized. The smart contracts are automated and needs no intermediaries.

3)Due to the lack of confidence of people on their currency, this has really boosted the success of defi.

4)Blockchain Oracle. A blockchain Oracle is any device that connects the blockchain to the outside world, hence it sends data from the blockchain to the real world and vice versa. e.g chainlink,flux.

5)Defi lending and borrowing. -Decentralized lending let's users lend their crptocurrencies to others to gain yields. Decentralized borrowers allows individuals to borrow money at a specific rate. e.g compound finance,marker Dao. Hint:Defi lending comes with it's own risk.

6)Decentalized exchanges. Dexes allow users to swap tokens e.g uniswap, pancake swap,curve,sushi swap.

7) Derivatives. This allows users to interact with assets without actually buying them (perpetual contracts). Defi derivatives allow users to use leverages,where users have the option to buy or sell (short or long)the market. e.g Dydx,Uma,Ribbon.

8)Yield Farming. A form of passive income on defi where defi users stake their crpto's to earn commissions which might come in form of other smaller tokens .

9) prediction. This are platforms where individuals can make predictions on the realization of future events like sports betting, politics , prediction of stock prices e.g Augur

10)Assets management. Defi asset management tends to make investment faster,less expensive and more democratized . This has high level of transparency. e.g Ampleforth.

11)Flash loans. Uncollateralized loans taken by people on defi protocols.
