Tinubu on Civil Service Reforms. #Thread / interview. “We brought computer experts to talk to them. I went to IBM, visited Oracle in the United States and Microsoft to look at which one would be a better database for the public sector accounting.” - Tinubu #FearlessTinubu

…. “Eventually we settled for Oracle and started developing some various other software on the database.” - Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu (@officialABAT ) #FearlessTinubu

“The payroll: to eliminate fake workers and change the payroll system was very, very challenging to equally account for those survey charges—let me take it to the micro level for a minute—was very, very challenging.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“For them to understand what my biometric system means to workers' ID, and you have to go to the bank: no more cash payments, no longer I commingle, non-accountable payroll system.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“But when they saw that the savings would become beneficial to their own need for better salary and better working conditions, they started responding.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“ I can recall the civil service test run of the payroll, and we eliminated thousands of ghost workers. We made it clear to them that, look, this is all that every one of you could share.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“…Only a very few people, fraudulent people, are taking it home pretending you have no money.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“Two hundred and sixty-something million in a month are regular pay workers. So we moved to a detection section where 7 of 451 hundred million naira in a month, eliminated ghost workers.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“By the time we finished with the local government and other workforce we were saving close to 600 million out of a payroll of 1.2 [billion]. So you see, close to 50%—more than 50% is subject of fraud and mismanagement of the payroll” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

ON INCENTIVES FOR CIVIL SERVANTS….. “First, increase payroll: rolling back those savings into increase in salary and allowances for them. Assisting them to develop interest in the financial market as a whole.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“Driving them to a savings that will be beneficial to their future. We had floated a bond—that’s a different story—for infrastructure.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“I asked them to see the magic of long-term investment and the return that will come from the interest rates, which is tax-free element of return.” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

“So this is the reward for them responding positively. Their living conditions, their office renovation, the congested office space, lack of hygiene in the toilets and so forth,…” @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu

…the working environment—you can’t imagine what it was some nine years ago with refuse all over the place” - @officialABAT #FearlessTinubu
