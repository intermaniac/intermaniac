Let’s talk a bit about the taurus/eclipse axis & the lessons of this season, especially with how much of what we’re feeling right now beneath the surface. so, the south node is in scorpio & the north node is in taurus. 🧵

when we’re working with the eclipses, we want to be grounded in the wisdom of the south node, while working towards the lessons of the north node. the south node highlights all that’s yet to be learned & that holds us back from new experiences. the north node is unfamiliar 🔻

but calling to us nonetheless. it’ll take some work to gain the blessings from it, but the more we listen to that call, the more we evolve within. unsurprisingly, with scorpio being about The Mysteries™ & all that fun woo woo stuff, this is a powerful time for diving 🔻

in to some new practices or wisdom, for sake of growth (taurus) after a profound transformation (scorpio) of some sort. individually, scorpio talks about our depths. our shadows: wounds, fears, pain, trauma, impulses, etc etc, all the “ugly” that we don’t really dive into 🔻

unless we have no other choice (or we have scorpio/8H placements, at which point it’s just another fun time 😉) symbolically, this is everything that exists beneath the surface - where we find the seed of something that can grow or the roots of something that already exists 🔻

taurus is the physical manifestation of that which exists within. our values, our desires, our self-worth, our finances, so on & so forth. taurus is the tree & all the branches/leaves that grow from it. uranus is also in taurus right now. 🔻

uranus is electricity, chaos, lightning, surprises, the unexpected, innovation etc. it disrupts the norm. it’s like a strong gust of wind, earthquake or lightning storm that’s shaking your branches. it’s all the things going on in the world around us, whether 🔻

directly related to us or not. it creates anxiety & uncertainty, which, inevitably, lift things up from beneath the surface. the primal nature of each & every one of us is being revealed in some way, alongside how we respond to those occurrences. none of it is 🔻

necessarily “good” or “bad” - it’s all humanity, in its rawest form. the surface level understanding is what will have us inclined to label experiences as “good” or “bad,” but that scorpio influence can call us to look even deeper for greater understanding. 🔻

so, as you’re navigating these times, think of a tree in a storm. sometimes we worry about whether the tree is going to make it & what type of damage it’ll endure, but moving past that feeling of uncertainty can help us to remember that there are roots beneath the surface 🔻

so long as those roots stay firmly planted in the earth, we know the tree will grow back eventually. that’s what we can work on applying to our own situations over the next two weeks. that solar eclipse is showing us what’s been eating away at our roots. we need them 🔻

healthy, if we’d like to make it through the storm. as we approach the lunar eclipse in taurus, we see what the manifestation is of all that’s going on beneath the surface. it’s not an easy time, but here’s the cool thing about eclipses: they’re not final. they’re temporary. 🔻

they invite change. they also take about 6 months to fully process in our reality. so observe what comes up right now. how you feel. what it brings up within & what exists around you as a result. tend to the poison & nurture the roots. your core. focus on what you 🔻

need to feel stable & grounded through uncertainty. however that resonates for you. taurus encourages acceptance & flexibility alongside uranus’ change & uncertainty, with scorpio’s diving within to regenerate & transform. reflect 🧘🏽‍♀️
