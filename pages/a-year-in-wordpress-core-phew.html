2022 — A Year in #WordPress Core ♥️ Phew, took me some hours to put together this😅 Read the full blogpost for a bunch of data about WP Core Contribution on Trac 📊 TL;DR ? I’m aggregating a few graphics in the thread below 👇 https://t.co/6gSGtZIELp

– In 2022, the WP Core team shipped 2597 commits (1852 in 2021) – 2656 tickets were opened, 2413 tickets were closed, and 353 were reopened. – 988 people contributed to WP source code using Trac (832 in 2021) – 398 people made their 1st contribution to WP Core (305 in 2021) ♥️

The most prolific components were: – Build/Test Tools with 366 commits (17% of all commits) – Docs with 297 commits (14%) – Editor with 207 (10%) – Coding Standards with 145 commits (7%) and Code Modernization with 135 (6%) – Bundled Themes with 128 commits (6%) https://t.co/dGQYq9KqUF

Top 5 countries by number of props received: 🇺🇸 United States with 1255 contributions (1142 in 2021) 🇷🇺 Russia with 1152 (227 in 2021) 🇫🇷 France with 739 (266 in 2021) 🇦🇺 Australia with 386 (211 in 2021) 🇮🇳 India with 317 (219 in 2021) https://t.co/s8uwu7tyNf

Here is the top eight countries by number of contributors (people involved) : 🇺🇸 United States with 152 people (155 in 2021) 🇮🇳 India: 77 (58 in 2021) 🇧🇩 Bangladesh: 43 (only 13 in 2021) 🇬🇧 United Kingdom: 38 (34 in 2021) 🇩🇪 Germany: 25 (23 in 2021) https://t.co/0P3G3xeKdZ

People from at least 229 companies were credited in WordPress Trac in 2022. Here is the top 5 companies by number of contributions: – @yoast with 1452 contributions – @automattic with 866 – @agence_whodunit with 676 – @10up with 501 – @bluehost with 226 https://t.co/xe4cZFPUD8

The graph below is also interesting, because it shows that a huge number of companies have only a very few core contributors on Trac. Some exceptions: @automattic (88 core contributors), @10up (38), @yoast (18), @WPDevTeam (15), @multidots (14) https://t.co/0W0s8QUj7Q

Now what about some 2022 stats about #WordPress Core Commiters? …you know, the people who are allowed to commit things to WordPress codebase 🛠 First, 32 Core Committers committed code to the WP SVN repository (33 in 2021 – yeah the number of active committers decreased 🤷‍♂️)

Commits by company in 2022: – 755 (39%) commits were made by core committers working at @yoast – 414 (21%) by @agence_whodunit core committers – 199 (10%) by core committers of @automattic – 128 by @10up committers – 125 by @bluehost committers https://t.co/S8DQN30OjQ

Number of WordPress Core Committers by company on 2022: @automattic has more than 10 active Core Committers. @GoogleOSS has 4 people allowed to commit code to #WordPress, followed by @10up and @humanmadeltd with 2 Core Committers each. The rest have 1 committer. https://t.co/bAblLjGBMz

Et voilà! That's all! I hope you'll enjoy these data :) Ha, one last thing: sorry for not putting any ALT text to these graphics. Instead of poorly describing them, I prefer to point you to the raw data, which should be easier to read: https://t.co/VjgPAvCItz
