Partial solar eclipse on October 25, 2022, if you live in Europe, west Asia, northeast Africa, and the Middle East you can watch solar eclipse tomorrow on 8:58 UTC (4:58 a.m. ET) and end at 13:02 UTC (9:02 a.m. ET). If you don't live in the above continents see the thread https://t.co/hSGtGgcEjZ

The Royal Observatory in Greenwich England will be live-streaming the partial solar eclipse via the Annie Maunder Astrographic Telescope. Thanks to astronomer Jake Foster. Watch here:- (https://t.co/CTVMU8nApS) Special thanks to @earthskyscience from where I got above information
