In these times of instability, it is important to remain calm and not believe in false gurus 🧙🏼‍♂️ selling us magical solutions 🤝 Oracles, nowadays, only exist on the blockchain 🤔❗❓ Yes, you read that right and today we're going to tell you what an oracle is 🧵👇 https://t.co/3XPaBuVQyu

1/ In Ancient Greece 🇬🇷 important figures consulted the Gods before making a big decision 🙌🏼 In more than one classical scene 📽 we have seen major personalities ask about their fate and the Gods, through oracles, delivered their message 📧

2/ But what is the relationship between oracles of Ancient Greece and the blockchain❓ Well, there is an analogy 👉 Humans, in isolation from their Gods, received information from them thanks to oracles 🤯 Blockchains are in a similar situation 👀

3/ Blockchains ⛓🧱 are isolated from the outside world Each block is connected only to the previous 🧱 and then to the following 🧱 Subsequently, bridges 🌉 were born, connecting blockchains to each other 🔂 But what about the connection with the outside world❓🤔 👇🏼

4/ This is where the analogy comes to life 👀 When a blockchain, like the Greeks 🇬🇷 needs to know information from the outside world, relies on an oracle 🤝 In this context, an oracle is in charge of communicating information 👉 🔒 Secure 🙌🏼 Trustworthy 🤝 Decentralised

5/ You may ask yourself, what information can a blockchain require from the outside world❓🤔 Let's imagine a #token that tracks the price of gold 🤝 Gold lives and forms its price outside the blockchain 🌏 To know its price, the blockchain must rely on an oracle 🤝

6/ So far everything is understood, but 👇🏼 How do oracles work❓🤔 Although many networks and projects have their own oracles 👀 let's take a look at how the most widespread oracle protocol in the crypto ecosystem works 👉 📣 @chainlink 📣

7/ To prevent attacks 🚓 that cause wrong information to be shared with a blockchain 👉 @chainlink developed a network 🕸 of nodes, which consult information from different sources⛲️ compare it and deliver a final result 🏁

8/ This final result is what the blockchain consumes 🤝 For example, in the case mentioned earlier 👉 After consulting information from various sources⛲️, @chainlink's nodes compare gold's price and deliver a final value, which will be assumed by the token 🙌🏼

9/ Naturally, at the present time it is extremely difficult to imagine 🤯 our crypto-ecosystem without the existence of oracles 🤝 In any case, as this is such a sensitive subject 🧪 both its development and its adoption ⚠️ must be extremely prudent ⚠️

10/ Attacks on different oracles have caused great losses to the crypto world 🙇🏽 In addition to other unfortunate events, we find ourselves 🤔 with a landscape that offers just a few options when it comes to oracles 🤷🏽 It is clear that those who survive are the ➕ strong ones

#Loopers 🙌🏼 In these complex times, it is important to stay calm and keep on learning 💪🏼 Remember that, as always 👉 Together, we learn better 🤗
