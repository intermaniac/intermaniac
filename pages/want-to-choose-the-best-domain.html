What is a domain name? A domain name refers to a website name. It helps users to find the website without remembering the IP address.

For example, this is the IP address of Facebook - 69.63.176.13. Instead of typing it on Google, you can just type “facebook” on Google and you’ll get the result. That means “facebook” is the domain name of the Facebook company.

‘www:’ Refers to all the public websites or pages that users can access from different devices through the internet. facebook: Second level domain (SLD), basically the name of your website. .com: This is the top-level domain (TLD). You have to register TLD along with SLD.

How to detect if it’s a good domain name? A good domain name- 1. Spreads the word about your business 2. Attracts walk-in traffic 3. Gives a professional feel to your business 4. Inspires curiosity, which can convert into a sale.

What are the different types of domains? .com: The best option for commercial websites. .org: If you have a non-profit business, .org will help you stand out. .city: A good option for brick-n-mortar businesses in a specific city, for example, .london or .boston.

.country: A solid choice for retailers who want to reach international markets. .shop: This is a good alternative for eCommerce websites. .store: A good option for small business owners and online retailers. .trade: A good choice for wholesalers or B2B eCommerce stores.

How to Choose the Best Domain Name for Your eCommerce Business? 1. Make the name unique & brandable 2. Keep it short & simple 4. Avoid hyphens & special characters 5. Try to choose .com TLD 6. Include keywords only if it makes sense 7. Choose a mobile-friendly name

What to do if you want to change your domain name? 1. Make sure your know why you are changing your domain name. 2. Backup your website content. 3. Save your backup file to various places. 4. Find out if your current host has transferring features.
