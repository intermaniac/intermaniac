Acquiring tech certs does not guarantee you a job! You need real world experience along with that cert. Many people are getting certs such as the AWS Solution Architect cert with no experience. Here’s one unique way you can gain experience to land a role as a SA. [thread]

AWS makes it so easy for architects and engineers to win by providing the tools and methodologies that you can use to help add value to businesses. AWS has a partner program that they created to help you innovate and expand your service offerings as a tech organization.

How you can use your AWS Solution Architect cert to gain experience is super simple. You need to work on actual projects, and how do you do that? Simply identify opportunities where companies are lacking and feel those gaps.

AWS gives you a really good service you can offer right away, and it's migrating workloads to AWS.

You’ll be surprised to know that a lot of organizations IT infrastructure is not currently in the cloud. Many organizations are spending a ton of time maintaining on-premise infrastructure rather than focusing on improving their operations.

Migrating an organizations IT infrastructure to the cloud can increase business agility, increase the efficiency of their development teams to focus on more strategic work, and provide global availability for resources.

AWS gives you the four pillars of cloud value framework to use when creating business cases for clients, you can use this tool to get the sale. The four pillars of cloud value consist of total cost savings, staff productivity, operational resilience, and business agility.

AWS also gives you a migration methodology that you can use as you start engaging with a client. There are three phases for the migration methodology and they are the assessment phase, the mobilization phase, and the migrate and modernization phase.

The Assessment phase is where you will build a business case and perform a cloud readiness assessment for clients. The Mobilization phase is where you will build on the AWS architecture and migration plan, and mobilize the customer’s team to migrate at scale.

The Migrate and Modernization phase is where you or your customer moves workloads to AWS, and a cloud operation model is put into place in AWS to run and optimize for further business benefits.

Migrating workloads to the cloud is how you can start your own practice and design architecture to put that knowledge that you’ve acquired by obtaining the AWS Solution Architect certification to use.
