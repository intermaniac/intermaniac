People remember good behaviors. People also remember lousy behavior. 9 things that make your customer service s*ck! - A thread #WordPress #ecommerce #business https://t.co/3zVzvXQFcg

1. Lack of empathy If you can't put yourself in your customer's shoes, it'll show! Showing empathy in problem-solving is the fastest way to win customers and make them lifelong loyal users.

2. No easy way to reach If you run a business but make it hard for your customers to reach you for their problems, it will be a nightmare.

3. Poor automation Your detailed, lengthy prompts will not make your customer happy. Make it short, actionable, and easy to digest

4. Waiting time Making your customers wait too long is a fast way to frustrate your users. I don’t like waiting on the phone for a solution for more than five minutes. Neither do you.

5. Transferring agents to agents When customers call you, they expect to get a solution in a short time. Users may be waiting but not for long, but making them wait while you transfer them from one agent to another agent will make the situation worse

6. Bad attitudes Agents with bad attitudes are the main reasons for most viral customer service stories you will read online. Just go to https://t.co/sb1VgyG57K, you'll see!

7. Not utilizing proper tools Your team is as good as the tools you give them. If you're #helpdesk isn't on par with the best, you're teams will suffer as will your customers!

8. No respect for customer’s privacy Some customers are willing to share more, and some can choose not to. You have to respect that as a business. Period.

9. Failed to respond in real-time According to Hubspot research, 90% of consumers rate immediate response as important when they contact customer support. In this digital time, people are accustomed to instant replies. Without it, are you even in the same century?
