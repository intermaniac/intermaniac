Why would I hire a web developer when I can just use Shopify/WordPress or any other web making tool? Well let me tell you why. THREAD 1/6

Tools like WordPress and Shopify are very powerful for creating fairly standard websites, usually shops and blogs. Many people don't need anything more than this. HOWEVER. THREAD 2/6

The free templates they come with, everyone else has them too. If you want to stand out, you will have to put some work into it. Might not be programming, but that's just one part of creating a website. What matters is DESIGN THREAD 3/6

What if you need something more complex than that? What if you want to create a web app that tracks a user's habits and recommends them products based on their needs? Or a store with a customer theme? Well then you'll need a developer. THREAD 4/6

With some code, you can build said app. You'll be talking to a real person who understands your needs, and wants you to succeed. And even if all you want is a custom theme, for let's say Shopify, most of us will be happy to do it for you also. THREAD 5/6
