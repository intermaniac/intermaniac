3/üßµ To reduce oracle price manipulation risk platforms may decentralize the off-chain sources being fed into the on-chain oracle. This is done by utilizing oracle feeds from reputable aggregators ($LINK) or pools w/ deep liquidity to increase the monetary cost of an attack.
