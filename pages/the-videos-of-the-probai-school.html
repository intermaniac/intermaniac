The videos of the ProbAI School 2022 (in Helsinki) are available on the YouTube channel. Here is a quick thread with each of them to share and spread on this network as well. (all are already linked in the GitHub repo) https://t.co/ErgTsf4qoJ 1/n

- Opening talk by Luigi Acerbi (@AcerbiLuigi), https://t.co/btptKq6jHq - What is Probabilistic AI? by Arto Klami https://t.co/n7UcRVPq4U - Introduction to Probabilistic Models by Antonio Salmerón (@ascerdan) https://t.co/cikazIQnYk 2/

- Probabilistic Modeling and Programming by Andrés R. Masegosa (@andresmasegosa) and Thomas D. Nielsen (@tdyhren) https://t.co/S3xQgURjfi - Bayesian Workflow by Elizaveta Semenova (@liza_p_semenova) https://t.co/S72tMnEJFE 3/

- Variational Inference and optimization (part 1) by Andrés R. Masegosa (@andresmasegosa), Helge Langseth (@HelgeLangseth) & Thomas D. Nielsen (@tdyhren) https://t.co/VgTl8W8CUA - Variational Inference and optimization (part 2), same lecturers https://t.co/fcNKFTWDII 4/

- Deep Generative Models by Rianne van den Berg (@vdbergrianne) https://t.co/YcBtUG9UZ5 - Normalizing Flows by Didrik Nielsen (@nielsen_didrik) https://t.co/2iWXnSnghz - Gaussian Processes by Arno Solin (@arnosolin) https://t.co/tLhxtkywf5 5/

- Neural ODEs by Çağatay Yıldız (@CgtyYldz) https://t.co/aDis2FVaer - Simulator-Based Inference / ELFI Tutorial by Henri Pesonen (@henri_pesonen) https://t.co/Go6MHHmeiF - Human-Centric ML by Fani Deligianni (@fd30) https://t.co/BC6lngZwX7 6/

- Bayesian Neural Networks by Yingzhen Li (@liyzhen2) https://t.co/dHTWGBgX1x - Advanced Bayesian Neural Networks by José Miguel Hernández–Lobato (@jmhernandez233) https://t.co/S8fkfoUhSm - Closing of ProbAI 2022 by Luigi Acerbi (@AcerbiLuigi) https://t.co/LARd7enUrg

Thanks again to all the lecturers, chairs, and organization of ProbAI. Thanks, @tarikssalem for editing the videos.
