Are you using Carp for your project? Carp, our new Cardano SQL Indexer and cardano-db-sync replacement has gotten over 40 stars on GitHub. Why is this significant, and why is Carp revolutionary? Find out in this thread:⬇️ 1/ https://t.co/THKStzBgsT

In Fund 8 of #ProjectCatalyst we proposed a replacement to db-sync in Rust powered by Oura. The proposal was funded, and we built and released Carp in May 2022. https://t.co/45r9TTSP0C /2

Carp has gotten over 40 stars on GitHub, which means that developers are either using it or saving it for later! This is great, but what are the benefits of using Carp? https://t.co/5thUj9BgBJ /3

Carp provides you with full data, instead of providing just bits and pieces like db-sync. Carp is revolutionary based on the following core pillars. Flexible, Modular, Speed, Typesafe and Documented. /4

Flexible: allow wallets and dApps full freedom in how they handle data /5

Modular: it should only index what you need /6

Typesafe: developers should know exactly what data to expect to avoid any critical issues in their products. /7

Documented: it should be easy for any developer to get started with Carp https://t.co/3FzNQmZsu1 /8

As you can see, there are many benefits to using Carp, but you can read even more about it here: https://t.co/vtkHL6H1Ml /9

As multiple companies and projects in the Cardano ecosystem are now using Carp, we have a new Catalyst Proposal in Fund 9 to help with its continued maintenance and development. Check it out here: https://t.co/45r9TTSP0C /10
