@screamingfrog Load the inlinks.xlsx file into pandas using: links = https://t.co/tMQVycc18p_excel(links_filename)

Remove duplicate links with from same source, link position and anchor texts: links_noduplicates = links.groupby(by=["Source", "Destination", "Anchor", "Link Position"], group_keys=False, as_index=False).agg({'Type': 'first' })

Group inlinks by destination, anchor and link position: links_condensed = links_noduplicates.groupby(by=["Destination", "Anchor", "Link Position"], group_keys=False, as_index=False).agg({'Type': 'count'})

Now you can select a destination url and check how many source pages link to the url under which anchor texts and which link position. This way you can identify potential for additional linking and improving your internal anchor texts to match your desired keywords ...
