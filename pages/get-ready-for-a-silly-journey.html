🐍🔁 Get ready for a silly journey through the different ways of controlling loops in #Python, with a Monty Python twist! It’s interactive so take opportunity to play with code in browser using #pyrun browser plugin! 🧵 👇 #MontyPython #Loops #programming #100daysofcode

0. Here’s what Gumby the village simpleton would do🤦‍♂️ Just don’t! Follow the examples and solve it in another way using the tips given, and you should be on your way to recursive greatness💪 🧠 #pyrun https://t.co/MK8ZWzaeyC

1. 🐍🔁 Now let’s get loopy with Python! Let's start with the classic for loop over a silly list of Monty Python quotes #pyrun made by @guilatrova💪 https://t.co/DwBeqmbOXO

2. 🐍🔁 while loops are great for knights that don't know how many times they need to say 'Ni!' They keep repeating themselves until a certain condition is met. Which, invariably never happens... Maybe a Palm Tree would do it 🌴? #pyrun https://t.co/rbhDNibzJ4

3. 🐍🔁 range() with for loop is perfect for counting dead parrots. It's a straightforward way of iterating a fixed number of times, for when Mr Death, or the plague, comes knocking ⚰️ #pyrun https://t.co/ddb9LYrZmA

4.🐍🔁 For loop with index, perfect for knighting fluffy bunnies like a proper loopy Monarch Get ready for a schedule of 8 AM Pointy-toothed Knighting Ceremony! 🐰🐰🐰 #pyrun https://t.co/AeGzTs4wcw

5. 🐍🔁 items() method to loop through dictionary can be used to find the Holy Grail. It's a great way to get key-value pairs and iterate through them, to find it...it in...Brexit Britain?!?!🤦‍♂️ #pyrun https://t.co/Dl2rKzRsmy

6. 🐍🔁 Recursion is a creative way to loop, like going down the proverbial rabbit hole. Be careful not to get stuck in an infinite rabbit-hole-loop! 🐇 ctrl+c/break is your friend 👍 #pyrun https://t.co/ruEQvH1X0r

7.🐍🔁 os.scandir() allows efficient looping through files in a directory. It's a great way to loop through a treasure trove of files, in search of the worlds funniest joke 🫖 ...Just don't read it 👍 #pyrun https://t.co/82vG0DjdBM

8.🐍🔁 enumerate() function gives access to both index and value when looping through terable Perhaps keep track of brave knights, Robin not really the ’leading from the front’-type, however Completely different to previous enumerate example, or maybe I like enumerate😉 #pyrun https://t.co/TxDimlIyFY

9. 🐍🔁 zip() is great for looping through multiple iterables in parallel, like holy hand grenades and their counts. Is it throw on 3, or... 🤔 #pyrun https://t.co/MES5ZoRPqB

10. 🐍🔁 itertools for unusual looping, like creating infinite sequences of 'Ni!'. A great way to loop indefinitely, if you hate trees...or shrubberies #pyrun https://t.co/Uu9xknw6ER
