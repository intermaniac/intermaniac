🌟 Revision success stories 🌟 A thread! My personal ones 🥹 Revision visualisation / affirmation / scripting!! All~

Revision using visualisation: - Revised the tone of a text by visualising my desired text tone. - Revised # of ppl in the store by visualising lesser ppl in it Truth is, I hardly visualise for revision.

Revision using affirmations! There’s a lot 🤣 Success 1: I manifested my severe period cramps away PERMANENTLY. I just go on an affirmation rampage ONCE due to pain that “I’ve nvr had period cramps. It will always nvr hurt, I don’t have period cramps, it nvr existed.” Gone 4ever

Success 2: There was once I had to pay for shipping agn cuz the mail got returned to sender. I was told to pay but I told myself “I don’t have to pay for it” but at the same time, I still went with the plan of paying. As I’m abt to pay, seller texted to say she’ll pay instead-

- because she made a mistake, tho it was my fault for incomplete add Success 3: I revised not selling a certain good to a buyer and resold it for a higher price. I simply scripted + affirm the person will back out, tho she was clearly wanting the item. 2 days later, she did.

Success 4: My sibling treated me rlly rude sometimes, keeps bringing up things I’ve said in the past. Instead of reacting to the 3D, I started revising saying they have always treated me nice etc. The next day, 180 degree change. Till today, they hardly even treated me rude

Success 5: Revised not wanting to deal with a certain buyer cuz she was being a pain in the a** so I said “I’m nvr selling to her, she didn’t want it anymore, I’m not dealing with her anymore” even tho she’s spam texting me. In a few hrs, her account deactivated LOL

Success 6: Anxiety. I know part of the reason that led to my fight and breakup is anxiety from past traumas. I revised nvr having anxiety and nvr having traumas that triggered it. Rampaged once and it’s been 2 days now, anxiety barely came up & I’m calm. I’ll continue revising

Revision using scripting! Like visualisation, I barely even script for revision. I mainly affirm because affirming and rampaging works so fast!! For scripting, I script my desired outcome (revised a falling out with a friend) and we reconciled

That’s all I can rmb~ thanks for reading and good luck to you all for revision ❤️
