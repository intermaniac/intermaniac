Tbh i think a lot of ppl's outrage with this episode comes down to a scripting issue. the series only started establishing dimitri's religiosity in ep 5, *after* showing he was against several elementalist policies (like the age law and live fire) ðŸ§µ #vampireacademy #vaspoilers

and nothing so far has indicated that, even tho dimitri *is* the type of person who would push rose away bc of duty, that he'd be the type to regress so far that he'd willingly support a royal reactionary who advocated for the policies he vocally opposed in the first half of s1

so it's a scripting issue with either ep 8 *not being clear enough* that dimitri doesn't know tatiana advocated for those policies *or* a scripting issue with the previous episodes not writing dimitri as the type of person who'd support a reactionary out of religious beliefs

based on dimitri's established character, the only way this episode works without being blisteringly ooc is if he was in the dark about the full scope of tatiana's plans and full policies and has some sort of existential "what have i done" crisis about it once he finds out

like, "has faith" isn't a shorthand for "is as conservative as the most basic stereotype of religious christians", there are plenty of religious people with progressive values and that's how dimitri was characterized eps 1-7. so the writing in this ep has ppl understandably upset

and confused wrt the direction the story's trying to go with him. the performances are solid as ever, but there's a *big* jump from "we can't be together bc duty" to "siding with tatiana" and the writing hasn't properly bridged that jump for dimitri's character in any way

like, maybe episodes 9 or 10 can save this?? but for now even tho dimitri regressing into "duty first" does make sense, the writing this episode has done such a 180 with his established traits that its hard to attribute it to anything but cheap, inconsistent drama writing

tbh it reminds me of network tv shows where different writers have radically different views on the same character so the character's personality changes between episodes based on who's writing. the va writers have been much better than this so far so it's disappointing to see
