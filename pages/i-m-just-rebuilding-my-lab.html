I usually have at least one instance of #Kali running in my environment. In the past I installed it in a Gen1 Hyper-V VM with 4GB of RAM and 4 cores. Everything fine.

But now this config results in a black screen of death after booting.

The solution: I installed Kali in a Gen2 VM with 4 cores and 4GB RAM. Additionally I made sure, that Secure Boot is turned OFF! The last step is to tell GRUB to load Kali with a Hyper-V display config. video=hyperv_fb:1920x1080
