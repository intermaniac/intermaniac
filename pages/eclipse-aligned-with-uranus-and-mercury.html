Eclipse aligned with Uranus and Mercury! Time for a thread on the astrology of quantum physics! Broadly speaking. In my research, this eclipse falls on what I've called the Quantum Degree. Read on to find out why.

Nov 8's Total Lunar Eclipse is at the 17th degree Taurus (counting from the first through thirtieth degree of a sign, anything in 16'01-17'00 can be considered the 17th degree, and that's how degree symbols are counted). The Pleiadian symbol is "Light and mirrors dancing"

This symbol reminds us of the famous "double slit experiment" which among other things can prove the dual nature of both matter and light as particles and waves. Light reflects off a mirror and passes through the slits, which display interference patterns. Like 17° Taurus.

And, the founder of the field of quantum physics- Max Planck (b. 4/28/1858) was born with a conjunction of Venus and Chariklo in Taurus: 16°36 and 16°49 - i.e., "17th Deg Taurus" - Light and Mirrors Dancing!

Cute! Well, before Planck, Nikola Tesla was born: 7/10/1856 - during a lightning storm at Midnight! I won't run thru his chart here. But something to share about our Eclipse degree: when Tesla "invented" the rotating magnetic field, which inspired many modern inventions... https://t.co/84tx5kuLhg

This degree was everywhere! (In modern timing techniques, that is). We don't have a specific date for the thought experiment, but it was probably in February 1882 (Budapest). For Tesla, his Solar Return of 1881 featured Neptune at that degree, conjunct Okyrhoe within 1°. https://t.co/g20IyTUYEH

Tesla's Lunar Return for Feb 1882 has Jupiter conjunct Chiron just a half degree past this symbol, at 17.5° Taurus. Okyrhoe and Neptune still conjoin, earlier in Taurus. BTW, Tesla preceded Quantum Physics. Here it's just a symbolic thread of invention.

Tertiary Progressions (When one day stands for a lunar month - instead of a year, as with secondaries - here = the date June 15, 1857) yields Venus conjunct both Chariklo and Okyrhoe, straddling the 17th degree (15° 20-18°01) of Taurus!

Lastly, with Minor Progressions (where a synodic month is equivalent to a year - see https://t.co/pdGNdoHYx4 for more info!) in Tesla's genius event, Mercury is RX at 16°18 Taurus, or 17th Degree, "Light and Mirrors Dancing." He drew in the dirt, and revolutionized electricity!

So while this eclipse seems ominous in its intensity, we can also draw on this historic and Uranian event to channel our Mercury-Uranus inspiration. What light will dance in the mirror of your mind?
