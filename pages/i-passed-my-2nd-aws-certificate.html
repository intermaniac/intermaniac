I passed my 2nd #AWS certificate within a week. Here are 5 Tips & tricks ðŸ§µ https://t.co/KxpqXngDDf

1/ Buy practice exams. They will teach you how to read questions. E.g. "minimal management of infrustructure" susggest answer with serverless service, "highly-available" suggests multiple AZ or Regions. @tutorialsdojo questions have better expalnations than e.g. @Whizlabs

2/ Buy AWS course. I went though AWS course and read AWS Developer Guides for key services (it takes a lot of time, but you can skip it). If you want to go deep just buy @adriancantrill course. If not, then go with @StephaneMaarek course.

3/ Take exam in test center I passed my 1st certificate in Vue test center, 2nd with online proctor. Exam app crashed 2 times during my online exam. I didn't check all my answers at the end, because I was worried that it may crash again.

4/ Schedule your exam in the morning I started my 2nd exam after 8 p.m. on Sunday. Due to technical issues it took me longer than expected. I was exhausted in the middle of the exam.

5/ Just try practice exam if you have experience with AWS If you have been using AWS extensively for many years (I have 4 yoe with AWS), you can just go through a few practice exams and check if you score above 72%. On the other hand you can learn a lot preparing to the exam.
