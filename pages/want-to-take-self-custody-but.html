Want to take self custody but don't have a second computer? Or maybe you're not comfortable with Linux? No problem! You can still have a secure airgapped Bitcoin setup on a budget and without technical skills. ðŸ§µðŸ‘‡

What is airgapping? It's completely separating your signing device from any internet connected device. USB and Bluetooth are attack vectors that can be taken advantage of. Not having a reliance on them is a huge step up with security.

First, download and install Bitcoin Core for your operating system. There are very simple instructions for verifying your download before you install. Really give it a shot to verify, it's important to know you're actually "Running Bitcoin." https://t.co/ijgpyY94fE

Next, download, verify, and install Sparrow Wallet. Sparrow is a simple-yet-powerful Bitcoin wallet that is easy for beginners and has all the tools you'll need as you continue down the rabbit hole. It's easy to connect it to Bitcoin Core locally. https://t.co/u2rHWSe1Wr

Now for the Airgapped Bitcoin Computer (ABC; h/t @shishi21m), I use @SeedSigner because I prefer building my own hardware, but Specter-DIY and @FOUNDATIONdvcs also follow the same principles. This is how you'll be able to sign txs without plugging anything into your computer.

Use your ABC to generate a new seed, import the xpub into Sparrow, and you can receive bitcoin. Send a test tx to yourself at a new address to get familiar with the process of signing from your ABC. The docs are a great place to get started learning. https://t.co/7v224NS1LQ

With everything set up and tested, you should back up your seed phrase in steel to make your setup disaster proof. I use @CodlStorage because their metal plates are compact, they have dimples for punching, and they're inexpensive.

You can also punch your seed phrase as a QR code in steel as well. I use @SteelQR21 for this so that I'm not reliant on a paper QR code when reading into the SeedSigner. This is optional, but convenient.

Last, don't be afraid to use support channels to get help. Telegram is great for this and there are plenty of people willing and able to help newbies get started with self custody. Because that's the whole point: take self custody!
