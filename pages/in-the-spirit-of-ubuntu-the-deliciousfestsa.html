In the spirit of ubuntu, the @DeliciousFestSA l and its partners are supporting Chefs with Compassion this Heritage Day weekend. We hope to raise R500 000 to help our volunteer network of community kitchens continue feeding those who have fallen on hard times. (A thread) https://t.co/n7kPlsdDXG

How can festival-goers support Chefs with Compassion? • Buy a care pack containing non-perishable food for R60 at participating Sasol Delight stores and at the Sasol Gazebo next to the Chefs with Compassion stand.

• Come to the Chefs with Compassion stand and meet the celebrity chefs who have come out to support our cause, sign plates that can be purchased for R50, and get social with festival-goers.

On Saturday you can expect to meet Chef NTI, Chef Wandile Mabaso of Les Creatifs restaurant, Chef Moses Moloi of Zioux restaurant and Chef Naledi - From My Kitchen To Yours, and on Sunday come and meet Cooking with Zanele and The Lazy Makoti.

• Treat yourself to a Heritage Day meal of delicious braaied meat in the Fire Pit alongside the Chefs with Compassion stand. For just R140 you’ll get a meal for two people, a delicious celebration of Braai Day PLUS R20 from every plate will be donated to CWC.

• Enter a raffle to win an exclusive commemorative magnum of red wine signed by some of the Delicious Festival artists, and the opportunity to savour sundowners on the Main Stage! There’s a one in 300 chance of winning this exclusive magnum of wine and tickets are just R200.

How can non ticket-holders support Chefs with Compassion? • Even if you can’t be at the Festival, you can still support Chefs with Compassion this Heritage Day. Donations are gratefully accepted and Section 18A certificates will be provided on request.

Please go to https://t.co/MzjWwvf1NU to make a donation. #compassion #feedbelliesnotbins #chefswithcompassion #donate #deliciousfestival #heritageday2022
