I'm curious to see how the WordPress Twitter thread functionality works. Here's my first attempt. A thread ⬇️ https://t.co/cZSUnYilY7

I didn't know you could use your WordPress blog to write Twitter threads or convert your existing blog posts into threads.

I've been dabbling with threads recently, mostly using Hypefury or Typefully to compose them. I want to write more micro-essays and turn them into threads without overwhelming my Twitter feed.

I do sometimes find threads hard to follow. I know you can read them through a thread reader like Readwise, but that just adds another layer of complexity to the process.

Anyway, I also understand WordPress will pull your images and videos through with the thread. So let me test that out. Here is a photo I took on my walk in the fields today: https://t.co/UFiEND8CbE

Last thing, you can work the other way away around and import and unroll threads into WordPress and turn them into blog posts.
