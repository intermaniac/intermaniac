Margin: 0 auto;("0" for top and bottom & "auto" for left and rigth) Can also use Margin-inline: auto; shorthand property for Margin-left & Margin-right.) basically "auto" takes out the white space and distribue it evenly on left & right side of element to Center it horizontally https://t.co/mojCicEPBR

Flexbox: To Center a div using flex-box Apply display: flex; to Parent Element With justify-content: center; to Center Child element horizontally and align-Items: center; To Center Child Element Vertically. https://t.co/Jujcnncl9F

Apply Display: grid; On parent element and Place-content: center; (place-content is shorthand property for justify-content and align-content) to Center Child Elements Both horizontally and vertically. https://t.co/TdyGppcZuf

Note : "Place-Items: Center;" Will Also Center This Child but These two properties work completely different I Strongly reccommend Checking out this tutorial for Place-content vs place-item by @KevinJPowell https://t.co/hFE6mWmzPU

Positions can also be used to center a div in a container. https://t.co/jNb1XZVbeZ
