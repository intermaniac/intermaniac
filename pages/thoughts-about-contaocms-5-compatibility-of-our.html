We've been involved in @contaocms extension development for way over 10 years now. Some of them have made their way into the core (ce_access for example), others have advanced to some of the most widely used and popular extensions there are.

5/10 of the most installed Contao extensions happen to bear our vendor name. We're proud of that because it means, we're contributing not only a big deal to Contao itself but also to its ecosystem.

Some of them are tiny, like folderpage. It's easy to make them compatible with Contao 5. In fact, this one already is. Others are not. Some of them are as old as our company is. Like the notification center for example. Or @isotopecommerce for that matter.

Others are a bit younger but only a little bit. 10 years are a very long time in software development. Nothing is the same as it used to be. Software architecture principles have changed. Possiblities have evolved. Skills have improved.

We are currently in the process of evaluating our strategy as to how we can move forward here. Many of our extensions are so old, they are in desparate need of a complete rewrite.

We know that many people in this community rely on our extensions for their projects. It's great that you use them! But we also get contacted a lot these days, asked about when things are going to be compatible with Contao 5. It's stressful because we don't know. We need time.

We need a LOT of time. We're currently setting the scene for notification center 2.0. This one alone will probably take weeks to get done. And it's like one out of our what...40 extensions? 50?

We'll get there eventually but we need you to be patient. And if you rely on our extensions, then maybe consider becoming a GitHub Sponsor of ours. So that we can use more of our daily working hours to invest in those extensions.

Alternatively, become a @trakkedio customer. It's our attempt at making Contao maintenance a breeze and you not only support us but you'll get a great tool in return!

Let's rely on each other. Thank you for reading. PS: The same applies for all the developers of all the extensions you are using for your projects. Consider donating there too! Let's move to Contao 5 together!
