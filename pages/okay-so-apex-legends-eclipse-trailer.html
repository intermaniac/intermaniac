OKAY so Apex Legends Eclipse trailer breakdown of what the next season holds (summary of my rambly thoughts from when we watched it on stream) Tweeting now so that I can say I called it #ApexLegends #ApexLegendsEclipse #Prediction ðŸ§µ

Catalyst's backstory is that she lives on Cleo with her friends, and is strongly opposed to Hammond's industrialization of the moon and/or its conversion to a site of the Apex games (leading her to lose her job). https://t.co/TWkc4Nkh4F

She sees Seer as someone who could've used his influence to help Cleo and Boreas, but has become a sellout and is in cahoots with Silva. This and their confrontation means unique voicelines between the two. https://t.co/HN3A5W9l3H

Potential unique voicelines also with Rampart (who she seems to get along with in the Stories from the Outlands short), Octane (Duardo Silva's son), and Bloodhound (because they're both enby? admittedly this is a stretch) https://t.co/alM0C1jcCp

Catalyst's tactical. Deploy ferrofluid on the ground, it slows enemies running through it (different from Ash tac; it affects multiple targets and doesn't fully snare so you can run out of it). Relocate abilities should counter this (Ash ult, Loba bracelet, Horizon lift, etc) https://t.co/7sp5ZZOcqO

IDK if this part here means that her tac can also be used to create a temporary barrier? This might just be lore stuff https://t.co/fl3LRTh0Yq

Catalyst's ult is deploying a high ferrofluid wall. Can't be mantled or scaled, but Horizon lift, Octane vert pad, etc. might go over. Enemies can pass through the wall but are greatly slowed and vulnerable. Wall mitigates damage, doesn't seem to have HP (similar to Gibby bubble) https://t.co/x3UAH0AFjj

This is interesting! Seer deploys ult but still looks confused, suggesting the ferrofluid wall negates his ult. It's possible that the wall negates ALL SCANS going through it. So deploying this big ass wall is a giveaway that you're there, but enemies can't tell exact positions. https://t.co/yV3RExM0Nu

Ferrofluid wall probably doesn't block audio like Rhapsody's ult (mainly because people will have a fit about No Audio Legends lol) No idea what Catalyst's passive is? Maybe just that she isn't slowed by ferrofluid herself or something like that

Not much new to say about the new map that hasn't already been said. Transit system looks dope! And looks like you can use weapons on them. Also there seem to be these points in the map with big turbines that can airboost you across voids https://t.co/XGuPXzr29r

And weapons! Probably no new weapons, despite what leaks say about the Fanatic or the Nemesis. Respawn wants to control the rate of new content lest the game gets too bloated too fast. So if they're releasing a new legend, there probably won't also be a new weapon.

Catalyst has a Rampage and Bloodhound has a Mastiff. I believe trailers traditionally don't feature care package weapons (only exception being Kraber in Season 2 trailer). Mastiff and Rampage have been in CP for 2 seasons. This suggests these weapons are returning to ground loot. https://t.co/BjzEQRmKVq

What will replace them? Can rule out Havoc, CAR, R-99, Volt, Wingman, EVA-8, Flatline, Devotion; we see these in the trailer. Weapons don't usually re-enter CP (shotguns seem to be exception). My prediction is new CP weapons will be L-Star and Peacekeeper (or MOZAM? imagine)

Gonna return here after the patch notes get released and I wanna see how correct I was lol! What are your thoughts? Agree? Disagree? Go to bed Chander it's 3am wtf?
