Our mission is to create the single source of truth for the world's real estate data.

Real estate information is extremely fragmented and difficult to access, which makes rapid innovation in the space nearly impossible.

We've spent years building in the proptech space and have experienced this problem over and over again.

Even something as simple as displaying data about a property on your website or app can take over six months, half a dozen FTE's, and cost over $100k.

Every company in the real estate industry across every vertical that wants to build something new must go through the same painful steps to access data before they can start.

For anyone who has worked in/adjacent to the space, it doesn't take long for it to become clear why real estate operates very much like it did in the 1850s.

Imagine if instead, accessing all of the information about any property in the world were as simple as looking up its address. That's Blockchain Home Registry.

The BHR protocol allows access to reliable information about any property in real-time (specs, appliances, maintenance, energy usage, etc.)

Here are some use cases of what could be built on top of BHR: Lending: faster approvals, auto-refinancing mortgages, DeFi lending

Ownership: fractionalized ownership, instantaneous property transfer

Title: instantaneous title checks and property rundowns

Retail: hyper-targeted marketing with access to real-time usage data

Insurance: more accurate underwriting, monetizing data with other firms

The list goes on... Simply put, BHR will provide critical infrastructure to the entire real estate industry and dramatically increase the pace of innovation any time it touches an address.
