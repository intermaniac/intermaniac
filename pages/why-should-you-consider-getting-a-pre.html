1) Ease of use: With a pre-installed Linux laptop, you can simply turn it on and start using it right away - no setup is needed whatsoever. You don't have to worry about installing or configuring the operating system to work with your hardware.

2) Compatibility: Pre-installed Linux laptops are designed to work seamlessly with the hardware and software, ensuring optimal performance and stability.

3) Security: #Linux is considered a more secure operating system than Windows or macOS. In general, Linux-based laptops are less vulnerable to malware and other cyber threats, making them an excellent choice for users concerned about #security.

4) Cost: #Linux operating system is #opensource, and it's free, so you don't have to pay for any license fees, unlike Windows or macOS.

5) Customization: Linux-based laptops are often highly customizable, so you can tailor the operating system to suit your specific needs and preferences.
