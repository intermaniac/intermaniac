WordPress has just crossed 500,000 members on @meetup with 777 groups and counting! 🎙️💙🤩💙 Besides free food 🍕 (check your local WP group) Here are 11 things that you can get out of your local WordPress meetup right now: 🧵 https://t.co/0mhk2ZrKRm

1 - WordPress meet-ups are for all job roles and skills. They are for everyone, new or old; business owners, organisation members, developers, marketers, and more! 🤩💫✨

2 - Find a place where you can chat with other people in your niche and improve your skills and value even further into that niche that you’re in.

3 - You can usually solve your website problems at a WordPress Meetup. Bring a laptop or share your website error or code. If not, then you will know someone who can solve your website problem for you! Your Network is Your Net Worth🚀🤑💰

4 - Depending on your local WordPress meetup, they can offer free drinks (alcohol or non-alcohol) 🍺🍻 - pizza or snacks suitable for your dietary requirements 🍕 - or your local cuisine 🤤 WordPress meet-ups can also serve food from your home kitchen 😉

5 - WordPress powers nearly 50% of every website out there (millions upon millions). You can get new and fresh ideas from people in similar niches, whether you talk about your role, challenges, stories, insights, or general banter.

6 - You can get fresh plugin ideas that can help your business, website, or your role as a developer. There are just so many plugins to choose from, that WordPress is pretty much the highest standard known to man for all the categories of plugins elsewhere on the internet! 🍃🌱

7 - You can make a new friend, network connection, or colleague at a WordPress meetup. That can turn into great social opportunities at no extra cost!

8 - Get in the know of what’s going on from within WordPress from the talk of the town. Usually, the latest information is distilled really well in your local WordPress meetup. So get a news bite before everyone tucks in. 🗞️💡

9 - Looking for inspiration?🎨🎨 That 1 conversation with someone can make all the difference to spark your next greatest idea, fuel your innovation, or help shape an existing idea. A WordPress meetup may just be exactly that.

10 - WordPress meetups last an hour or so. And in that space of time, you will almost always get a great experience with the open-source world!

11 - You can get out of a WordPress meetup - free merch and goodies! https://t.co/lSRIbvRv1o
