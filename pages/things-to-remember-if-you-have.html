1. It's important not to become entangled in semantics and going off topic about small details. Even if your audience is technically savvy, most people aren't experts in everything related to data analysis and data science Not presupposing too much knowledge on their part

2. Take advantage of simplifications - When explaining complex concepts, it can often be helpful to simplify them down into more easily understood terms. Avoid using complicated terminology when describing your data analyses or visualizations onstage

3. Many companies have terms and phrases that are specific to their workplace, and using them can make your explanations more resonant with your audience.

4. Lastly don't be a pedant and be patient when communicating with others.
