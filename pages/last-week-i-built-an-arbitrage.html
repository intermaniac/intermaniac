Last week I built an arbitrage bot for @ZeitgeistPM and made 30 USD. This week, the bot is obsolete. Over the weekend a new contender has arrived and he is taking it all now. The small tale of how I finally started learning @typescript, @PolkadotJs & arbitrage botting ðŸ§µ

Last week, @ZeitgeistPM launched. There are 2 pools in ZTG: 1) The prize pool, aka the bounty 2) the liquidity pool; where you buy outcome tokens I know from @CryptoCowboyOG how he found a very simple arb opporunity between the two pools during beta.

When all prediction tokens are balanced, their value sum is ~1 $ZTG The liquidity pool is very shallow, because liquidity provision is likely to be net negative. Which means token sums can deviate significantly from the value of 1 $ZTG. This is the arbitrage opportunity! https://t.co/OndsJc1jC9

The prize pool is interesting. It has an invariant that could be roughly described as x = y (I'm not a mathematician) You can buy an infinite amount of outcome tokens at the prize pool for an equal amount of $ZTG and vice versa. So price between pool differs!

Since you can source unlimited tokens from the prize pool, you can always arbitrage the liquidity pool to reach balance at a token sum of 1 $ZTG Could I build a bot around that idea?

All the tooling in the @substrate_io universe is built around @PolkadotJs and @typescript, so I had to pick those up first. The @ZeitgeistPM Github Repo is also gold: https://t.co/cGnHjyugtW

I put together a proof of concept in a day and was super excited for my first automated arbitrage and 0.6 cents of profit! https://t.co/nFrI1LzfnW

The script was very basic, but it worked. I had built a few small cloud projects in the past, so I took another day to deploy it in @googlecloud. https://t.co/NE1DHnSWmR

The profit kept rolling in while I was sleeping and I couldn't wait to get up in the morning to check how much I made https://t.co/agCCCVKA1l

I was already dreaming big. Can I deploy to other markets? Surely there is a ton of competition out there? But how established is it in Dotsama? Could I still be competitive? Then on Sunday, my rewards stopped coming in...

I tested my suspicion and it was true. A bot was following up my tx in the block right after a regular tx. That's a bit more elaborate then my proof of concept that runs every 5 minutes to see if there is an opportunity... https://t.co/xq9FFxAxJv

My bot is very inefficient. It doesn't know the exact amount to arb, I didn't do the math yet. I'm just guessing with a few very simple params. Which led to me making 30 USD profit over the range of 1475 extrinsics. https://t.co/hLQeiCgs5W

My competitor on the other hand works very elegant. Only 22 extrinsics for about 2 USD profit. https://t.co/9JcU9R7ZQb

well. I think I won't afford the time to outcompete them in the near term. I would see one or two attack vectors that are still open, but I think I'd rather focus on doing analytics again. Thanks for listening :)
