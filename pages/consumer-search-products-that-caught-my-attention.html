15 Consumer Search products that caught my attention in 2022 (aside from ChatGPT) ðŸ§µ - You - Consensus - Neeva - Perplexity - Hello - Looria - Rewind - Lexica - Tasmania - Recoll - AnyPod, Podly etc - Needl - PDFDrive - Metaphor - Github CodeSearch You can try em all NOW 1/10

You (you .com) has launched GPT-powered YouChat, a developer platform and a code-focussed search product. Consensus (consensus .app) is search across research papers, a competitor to Google Scholar. 2/10

Neeva (neeva .com) is the ad-free, privacy-first search engine. Perplexity (perplexity .ai) summarizes search results from Bing and allows complex SQL-like natural language queries on Twitter data, using GPT-3. 3/10

Hello (sayhello .so) is developer-focussed search engine that uses LLMs to answer code-related questions instantly. Looria (looria .com) is a product-focussed search engine that tries to understand product sentiment from Reddit and other review sites 4/10

Rewind (rewind .ai) indexes and compresses everything on your desktop ever and lets you search through it. Recently opened their beta. Lexica (lexica .art) lets you search through prompts and images used for generative AI engines like SD/MJ/DallE. 5/10

Tasmania (tasmania .sievedata .com) searches through a specific moment in a video across all of Youtube. Recoll (lesbonscomptes .com/recoll) does full-text search across all the documents on your desktop (not just titles) 6/10

Various forms of search and summarization across Podcasts. - AnyPod (anypod .ai) - Podly (podly .ai) - Voilib (voilib .com) - Specific: Huberman(huberman .rile .yt) + All-In (all-in-on-ai .vercel .app) 7/10

Needl (needl .tech) is a desktop app that indexes your connected cloud sources and your desktop files. PDFDrive (pdfdrive .com) searches through over 80m eBooks and PDFs on the internet. 8/10

Metaphor (metaphor .systems) is a universal LLM-backed generative search engine. Github Code Search (cs .github .com) is the newest code search from Github that is a 10x improvement on their previous search. 9/10

I omitted search platforms, search infra, enterprise search products, products I didn't enjoy using and vaporware. While the biggest thing in Search is LLMs like GPT, there's a lot more groundbreaking new work happening in the industry. 10/10
