tldr: Here's the link https://t.co/CUNjLFHXQM (click on the badges to see the extension in the marketplace) Continue the thread for more information (2/8)

The Starkware team is doing a great job in developing the Cairo language and the Starknet layer2. Right now the team is focused on the next improvements (regenesis, cairo v1...) much more important than the development of the vscode extension, which is fine. However, (3/8)

since the development of the vscode extensions that live in the cairo-lang monorepo is non-priority, the improvement of the extension is slowed down. This attempt is a way to relieve the Starkware teams by out-sourcing the development of the vscode extension. I don't (4/8)

think that the cairo-lang monorepo is the right place to host a product like this one whose development dynamics and code severity is different from the language itself. (5/8)

This extension is intended to support all the features developed by Starkware up to now, and to add to it features that make the development of programs written in Cairo smoother. At this time, all functionalities implemented in the official extension you use every day (6/8)

are included in the extension I suggest. Additionnaly, 10 new features have been added so far to make Cairo development smoother. That includes 7 new snippets (including @view, @constructor, struct...) and better parenthesis/curly braces management üéâ (7/8)

The extension is already available, feel free to test it, enjoy the improvements, and share your feedback! (8/8)
