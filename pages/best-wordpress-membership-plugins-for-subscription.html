14 Best #WordPress Membership Plugins for Subscription Sites (2022) 🧵

@MemberPress is an all-in-one WP login-based plugin. It streamlines building fully-featured @WordPress membership sites. 👉 You can create an unlimited number of customizable memberships. https://t.co/lMtgXkqyX8

OptinMonster is a premium WordPress plugin & the world’s #1 conversion toolkit. Using a WordPress membership plugin or subscription plugin is excellent, but only if you have members. While OptinMonster isn’t a login plugin, it can help get more users to your membership site. https://t.co/PmUu5fIBqY

@WooCommerce Memberships lets you limit content and set up recurring or one-time payments. This program lets you have a purchasing club. 👉 This means visitors have to sign up and log in before buying. https://t.co/XJ3U4vIFkq

Restrict Content Pro promises a simple setup. Then, you get robust content restrictions, premium content capabilities, and more. 👉 Restrict Content Pro offers plenty of payment options. These include @Stripe, https://t.co/SxdOyPqteq, and @PayPal. https://t.co/VnmWwf6fP1

aMember is a comprehensive yet simple membership plugin for WordPress. It uses PHP, but it lets you connect with WordPress anyway. 👉 PHP is great for many things, especially if you’ve updated to the current version. https://t.co/A1dBhT51EP

@S2Member is one of the best free membership plugins for WordPress and also offers in-depth paid versions. 👉 The pro version grants access to a comprehensive membership plugin. https://t.co/isK1Kt1uJc

Paid Memberships Pro has 100,000+ sites & been around for over 10 years. 👉 You can have unlimited WordPress membership levels with flexible pricing. It lets you offer coupons, discount codes, drip content, & more. 👉 You can have thousands of members, create communities, etc. https://t.co/puPZ4zX8Ei

@MemberMouse lets you sell protected content and customize access to each piece. 👉 This plugin lets members access more than online content. You can also sell products. https://t.co/sJDAY9XBoJ

WishList Member is all about customization. This plugin should work with most WP plugins, themes, and page builders. It also lets you set up trigger actions to increase efficiency. 👉 If you want an LMS, exclusive community, or private site, WishList Member can help. https://t.co/uf0JtWhobQ

WP-Members is a free WordPress membership plugin. You don’t need to change your current WP setup for it to work. 👉 They have a video and user guide to walk you through installation. It has a good number of features, but since it’s free, it’s a bit lighter than some others. https://t.co/LFLdRg0zDi

ARMember is a free WordPress members plugin with a range of features. 👉 You can create unlimited membership levels, 1-click content restrictions, and more. It also has a form and signup page builder. https://t.co/4t2pcEoEEF

Almost 90,000 sites use @MagicMembers. It’s also existed and kept maintained for over 10 years, showing its developers believe in the plugin. 👉 You can see this faith even more by its reasonable pricing; this means they want lots of people to be able to use it. https://t.co/Zj8ftzFV53

@LearnDashLMS is the #1 #LMS (learning management system). It isn’t a run-of-the-mill plugin. It lets you build a WordPress membership site for learners. Learners can be of all ages and backgrounds. 👉 This login-based plugin is considered one of the best in the LMS industry. https://t.co/rGeFzyVr7n

@bbPress is a free WordPress membership plugin. It is a forum software by the same team that made WordPress. 👉 You can add members to forums that you moderate. This allows them to have a community that shares ideas while you keep an eye out for things like hate speech. https://t.co/8GiiVKmMpX
