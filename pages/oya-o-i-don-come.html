This #viz is for Liverpool fans and it is based on the iconic 2004/2005 UCL final in Istanbul, Turkey. Liverpool, against all odds, came back from a 3-goal deficit to level the game in regular time and then win 3-2 on penalties.

This #viz shows all the shots taken during the match, grouped by the shot outcome. The white and red circles represent shots taken by AC Milan and Liverpool respectively. The size of the circles tells us the probability of a shot leading to a goal.

The data was obtained from @statsbomb through their R package 'StatsBombR'. Guess what? Pythonistas, you can also access the data through 'StatsBombPy' for Python.
