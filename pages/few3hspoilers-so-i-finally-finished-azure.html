#FEW3Hspoilers So I finally finished Azure Gleam and wanted to give my thoughts. There will be some spoilers so please mute this thread if you need to

Let's get the negatives out the way 1st. Some things were either never explained or brought up once then never happened again (Shez's origin, Hegemon Edelgard, Sothis possessing Byleth)

The ending felt very abrupt and the way Edelgard was just left, considering they know she's brainwashed, made the Zahras chapter feel pointless. Speaking of the extra chapters you get via recruiting Byleth, I wish she'd been playable in them and that she'd been available earlier

Now onto the positives. Shez was an entertaining protagonist, while I still prefer Byleth, he did win me over and I'm glad they didn't try to make him a replacement. Byleth played the role of temporary antagonist well and playing as her was a treat (she's very OP haha)

Rest of the Blue Lions cast stayed true to how they were in 3Houses. While it hurt to see Dimitri never learn to live for himself here, I did like how the others rallied around him and helped him to rely on them more

I'm not one who cares for graphics but this game is definitely a step up from 3Houses. While I did kinda miss the anime cutscenes, what we did get was fantastic. Gameplay wise, it's exactly how I expect a Warriors game to be, everyone felt unique and fun to play

Overall I really enjoyed Azure Gleam. I'm looking forward to see what the other two routes have in store. I may also do a route without recruiting anyone (I hear it changes the end) but we'll see
