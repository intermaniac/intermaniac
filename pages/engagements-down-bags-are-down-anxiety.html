The irony is, this is what many of you wished for when shit was booming. "If only i'd bought when Eth was below $2K"

It takes true conviction to keep buying while you witness everyone getting rektd.

Reality is, everything is on sale right now. Why did you like Eth at $4K but hate it at $1300?

Pessimism and FUD reign supreme right now, which makes it the ultimate buying opportunity.

Problem is, no dopamine. No quick hit of adrenaline.
