It's no secret that the traditional 9-5 work day is slowly becoming a thing of the past. As more and more people are embracing the digital age, they're also finding new and innovative ways to work outside the office. 2/12

One of the most popular methods of working remotely is what's known as "telecommuting." Telecommuting simply refers to working from home or another location outside of the office. 3/12

This arrangement has become increasingly popular in recent years, thanks to technological advances that make it easier to stay connected and productive outside of the office. 4/12

There are a number of benefits to telecommuting, both for employees and employers. For employees, telecommuting can offer a greater sense of work-life balance. It can also be a more cost-effective way to work since there's no need to commute daily to and from an office. 5/12

Employers also stand to benefit from telecommuting arrangements. Employers can save on overhead costs such as rent and utilities by allowing employees to work from home. 6/12
