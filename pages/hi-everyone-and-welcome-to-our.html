“Hi, everyone and welcome to our first Oracle Cerner Health Conference—and our first with in-person attendees since 2019.” Oracle Health Chairman @dtfeinberg kicks off #OCHC22, with thousands attending in Kansas City and tuning in virtually from around the world. [THREAD 🧵👇] https://t.co/NuDFlgc781

“As I stand here, I am excited, but I also feel an overwhelming sense of gratitude... It’s been a privilege to work with many of you and our great associates. We are all blessed to do work that matters.” Oracle Health GM @Travis__Dalton at #OCHC22 https://t.co/bp79J6bh0E

“I would like to thank all of you for your dedication to keeping your communities healthy, and looking after our families, friends and neighbors during an extraordinary time. Healthcare is personal and always has been.” @Travis__Dalton at #OCHC22

"Venues of care are shifting. Consumer expectations are changing. Operating expenses are growing. Cerner has shifted from a local KC company to the biggest EHR provider in the world. And we are now together as Oracle Health, with global reach and scale." @Travis__Dalton #OCHC22 https://t.co/njqtusjACa

“What hasn't changed is our commitment to listen, understand, and serve you. I'm as excited as I have been in my 21 years here. What I am most excited about is our ability to activate, start work with Oracle scale and execute with velocity to completion.” @Travis__Dalton #OCHC22 https://t.co/SjFGHC650W

At #OCHC22, @Travis__Dalton outlines our goals: • Improve the Clinical Experience: get the right information to the right person at the right time • Modernize Administrative Solutions: improve financial performance • Advancing #Interop & #PopHealth: using data to advance care https://t.co/cFjmHxaVuc

“We all know the statistics, and many of us have felt these numbers. As a third-year medical student, I remember an attending physician telling me about the challenges caregivers face. Burnout has been endemic in healthcare for decades.” @NasimAfsarMD takes the #OCHC22 stage https://t.co/BQg9TLS1SN

“We’ve seen an explosion of medical knowledge, new diagnostic and therapeutics, accountability for quality and patient experience, and new regulatory requirements. These advancements are important and necessary, but add to the complexity.” @NasimAfsarMD at #OCHC22

“While we added responsibilities onto the care team, we haven’t always equipped them with the right resources and tools. The result? More mental strain. More burnout.” Read how we're creating a more collaborative workspace for care teams. #OCHC22 https://t.co/25hOVh05ga

“If designed and leveraged appropriately, [technology] can reduce burnout by helping care teams collaborate more efficiently and find relevant information quickly. Technology can and should support caregivers—just as they do for patients.” @NasimAfsarMD at #OCHC22 https://t.co/ONe7fVbXsh

“Inaccurate and incomplete problem lists lead to quality and safety issues, as well as delays in care. The new problem summary is a game-changer." At #OCHC22, @NasimAfsarMD discusses our work to streamline care team workstreams. https://t.co/YbcXJsojdU

“Data abundance which can mean information overload, especially for caregivers at the point of care. Seamless Exchange aims to solve this challenge by removing the noise, transforming and organizing the data in more meaningful ways.” @NasimAfsarMD #OCHC22 https://t.co/d3CgJrm9U5

“As @dtfeinberg said, we can go beyond digitizing the paper record. We can bring technology and data to address all aspects of how care is provided and delivered in a more open and more connected way.” Oracle Health SVP @Brenna__Quinn at #OCHC22 https://t.co/tcnpyt22lT

“As we tackle post pandemic challenges like supply chain and workforce gaps, having the ability to understand patient care demand and the required resources and staff to meet it is essential.” Learn how we’re addressing workforce challenges. #OCHC22 https://t.co/kAhAd3jrSX

"RevElate is designed to make the complex billing process less complicated, less time intensive and more successful for your organization... and for the unsung heroes in your billing offices.” Read more on our patient accounting solutions. #OCHC22 https://t.co/18z8TMtOyg

“A common theme today has been the power of data—but it's really only great when usable. We want to simplify the use of data to make it more impactful.” On the #OCHC22 keynote stage, @Travis__Dalton shares how we’re “empowering care teams with usable information." https://t.co/ThiW8s31wD

“The only way to compete & win the battle [against cancer, diabetes, and other chronic conditions] is with data and intelligence. The right data, at the right time, in the right place is crucial for care.” @Travis__Dalton at #OCHC22 Learn more here 👇 https://t.co/9hv8BnBJSq

“Clinical factors aren’t the only influence on a person’s health and wellness. Where you live by country, by state, and even by neighborhood makes a profound difference. That’s why it’s important to bring social determinants of health into the EHR." Christy Dueck at #OCHC22 https://t.co/KqA10xmydj

“Having accurate, localized community data within the EHR is crucial to addressing social risk factors and improving community health. This same data can also be used to advance health equity—a specific focus of many of our health systems here in the US.” - Christy Dueck #OCHC22

“Just like we can use EHR data to address social risk factors and healthcare disparities, we can use EHR data to accelerate clinical innovation and democratize access to clinical research for all.” At #OCHC22, Christy Dueck discusses how to make clinical trials more inclusive.

"It’s amazing what we can do when we love our communities and use data to care for them and serve them... It’s all about people—at the center of it all is providing better care for patients." Oracle Cerner Chairman @dtfeinberg concludes the #OCHC22 Keynote. https://t.co/opve9LALI8
