4 - i could easily be akk, i relate too much to him and there are things that make me feel so close to him but at the same time i would be ayan too, if i see injustice i can't stay silent.

5 - to ayan everything started as a joke, a teasing game with a precise purpose but slowly akk became someone too important to him, he started seeing him from a different perspective and once you hit the first wall it's hard to stop, right?

5.2 - akk, well it's kind of the same, the both of them approach eo for a purpose but akk fell in love with him bc to me he saw someone different, someone who made him feel new things, someone that was there when he needed, they both learnt a lot from eo and im proud of them.
