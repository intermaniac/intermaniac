Okay so, I did a weird thing today and was someone's strange encounter. A ðŸ§µ. I decided, less than an hour before businesses close, that I wanted leather bags to hang off my belt at gencon to hold my oracle dice and alleymans tarot. I google 'Leather Bags near me'.

My first thought was to find a place like a Ren Faire leather stand, as they always have bags I think would be good. Guess what, there's one called Renaissance Leather in Denver! Sounds perfect. I grab the address, see they close at 6. It was 5.10. It would take 20 to get there.

So I decide I'll take my items, go there, see if a bag fits. If it does, Ill buy it. If not, Ill leave, so I don't hold them open or be obnoxious around closing time. I pull up at the building and there's no clear front door. I circle around. No, there's one. Kinda sketch tho...

It has a directory of businesses next to it, I figure it's one of those hidden away set of specialty stores. People only go there when they have specific reason to go, so that seems reasonable. The door is unlocked, so I go in.

Hey, this is definitely some kind of manufacturing shop, and it's definitely not leather. I see a man with headphones walking further in, and follow him until I can get his attention, now MUCH further into a different part of the workshop.

"Can you point me to Renaissance Leather?" He nods, points back deeper into the shop at some stairs. "Up and the door is behind you." I can tell now that this is not a place I am meant to be. This is not a hidden store, it's their communal workshop space.

I decide the worst thing that can happen is I say hi and they ask what I'm doing there, then I'd leave. So I go up and open the door, walk right in their shop. An older man looks at me very confused. I ask if this is Ren Leather, I wanted to buy a bag. He asks how I got in.

I shrug and say the door was unlocked. He says it shouldn't have been. He sees I'm serious about the bags, I have things I carried with me to put in the bags to see if they'd fit. "So do you have bags?" I ask, he nods. We go look at bags.

We find some that fit perfectly, but they don't have hardware on them. I note that because it's so late, and he was about to leave, I could come back the next day to pick up the bags when they are finished. He says "nonsense, it'll only take 10 minutes." We hang out for 20.

Terrence is a real nice dude. We chat about business, trade business cards, talk about maybe working on something together some time. He noted that the name on my card was Vanessa, said that probably wasn't me. I said it was.

He put my new leather bags in an old ubereats, yellow paper bag from some restaurant he had around the shop, and we leave together as he closes up. And he watches while the weirdest person he's met, a seeming man named Vanessa, gets in his car and leaves.

There's no moral I'm just fucking weird.
